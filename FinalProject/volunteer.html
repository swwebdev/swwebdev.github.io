FAQ:

Where can I find information on past safety incidents?

Here at Cosmic Critters, we're dedicated to the safety of our volunteers. That said, accidents are bound to happen, and we make a point of staying accountable.

Records of all past incidents can be found here.

Will I be provided with adequate PPE?

All necessary equipment will be provided to our volunteers. We do not expect you to bring any of your own equipment.

In fact, we recommend bringing as few belongings as possible. Our studies have shown that volunteers unencumbered by wordly possessions are much more efficient workers.

How much radiation will I be exposed to?

Radiation exposure will vary, however we guarantee that it will be below the lethal limit.

Repeat volunteers are urged to seek regular screenings with their medical providers.

Will I be compensated for the cost of my space travel?
