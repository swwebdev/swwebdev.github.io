Rocket's main mode of transportation are her trusty jet boots. Hold the right mouse button to blast off and point the cursor in the direction you want to go. The further you point the cursor, the faster you'll fly. Use the right mouse button to punch enemies that get in your way.

CHARACTERS

ROCKET is a fearless girl who loves adventure. With the help of her grandma's trusty gadgets, she sets out to defeat the alien invaders.

Does Rocket have what it takes to save the world? There's only one way to find out!

GRANDMA is the brain behind Rocket's brawn. Using her many years of technological expertise, she'll create gadgets for you to use throughout your journey.
